# About Waypath

Waypath is designed with precision, usability, and user autonomy in mind.

---

## Purpose

Waypath provides reliable navigation assistance for safe exploration and outdoor orientation.

The app is built to support users in forests, trails, and unfamiliar environments while maintaining full control in the userâ€™s hands.

---

## Key Principles

- No automatic actions are performed without explicit user input.
- All navigation points are stored locally on your device.
- Optimal point saving occurs when sensor accuracy is below 10 meters.
- Offline navigation remains fully functional.
- Full map rendering features may require internet connectivity.

---

## Compliance & Safety

Waypath complies with relevant privacy, safety, and industry regulations.

Users are advised to always follow:

- Local rules and laws
- Safety practices
- Personal judgment and awareness

Waypath is intended as an aid, not a replacement for professional tools or emergency systems.
