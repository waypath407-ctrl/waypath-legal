# Privacy Policy — Waypath

Effective Date: February 2026

Waypath is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring strong data security.

---

## Privacy & Data

Waypath only accesses your device’s location to provide navigation guidance and orientation features.

- No personal information, identifiers, or sensitive user data is collected.
- No location data is transmitted to external servers.
- All saved points and navigation history remain stored locally on your device.
- No login, account registration, or cloud backup is required for full functionality.
- Internet access is optional and used solely for enhanced map rendering when enabled.

Users retain full control over their data and are responsible for managing device privacy settings.

By using Waypath, you consent to local device data usage only.

---

## Data Sharing

Waypath does **not** sell, share, or distribute user data to any third parties.

---

## User Control

You may disable location permissions at any time through your phone’s system settings.  
However, navigation features may not function correctly without location access.
